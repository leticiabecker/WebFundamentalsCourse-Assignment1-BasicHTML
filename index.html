Did you know?

For the epic two-minute solo that closes Alive, lead guitarist Mike McCready tried to channel one of his childhood heroes: Kiss ax man Ace Frehley. Ace was their firecracker, their dynamite McCready wrote in Rolling Stone Magazine in 2014. He's what took them over the top with the feel he put into his leads. I really gravitated towards his vibrato. My lead for Alive is based on She and that's based on Five to One by the Doors.
